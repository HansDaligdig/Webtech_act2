Latest Event

For the 29th ASEACCU International Conference, Saint Louis University, located in Baguio City, Philippines, was chosen to be the host institution for the said event. Having the theme "Global Goals in the Core of our Common Home: A Catholic Response," SLU held the event from August 21 to August 25, 2023 with the ASEACCU Secretariat along with over 200 faculty and students from fellow member-countries. It served as a platform for international educational dialogue through lectures, conferences, cultural exchanges, and exposure site visits.

Mr. François Mabille served to be the keynote speaker for the said conference. He is the founder of the Research Centre on Peace with Joseph Maïla, a statutory researcher of the Societies, Religion and Secularism Group of the National Center for Scientific Research (CNRS), and the Secretary General of the International Federation of Catholic Universities (IFCU). Joined by fourteen other speakers, Mr. Mabille's central address, “IFCU, A Global Tool for the Care of our Common Home," challenged perspectives as well as inspire participants. The conference also consisted of excursions to Banangan Women’s Association, Mount Costa, and Mirador Heritage and Eco-Spirituality Park; accompanied with a Student Night event featuring presentations by student participants of ASEACCU member schools and the SLU Center for Culture and the Arts (SLU-CCA) Performing Arts Group.
