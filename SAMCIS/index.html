What is it all about?

Career development describes a complicated process of planning activities and engagement in one’s career to establish growth in their chosen career. Such growth is valuable to both the individual and the company as it contributes to the progress of the individual in reaching their career objectives through the development of their skills, capabilities, decision-making, competence, and comprehension. It encompasses the need for understanding one’s career and its depictions as well as knowledge management and effort to achieve one’s personal desired future.
